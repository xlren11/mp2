Hand Pulled Noodle

The best pulled noodle is in Lanzhou China. The chefs pulled out the noodel from the dough using their hands. Thus pulling the noodle itself is kind of showmanship. Skilled cook can pull noodle into various shapes to provide different texture. Compare with machine made noodle, pulled noodle is elastic. The topping is usually beef slice and parsley

Japanese Ramen

amen is widely known as famous Japanese representative noodle, it's originated from China and Japanese chefs made changes to it. Ramen noodels are normally machine made. The soup is the soul of Ramen, Ramens are usually categorized by the ingredient from the soup. Also topping is another highlight of Ramen, popular toppings are half cooked eggs and sea weed

Hand sliced noodle

Sliced noodle is sliced directed from the dough by skilled chef using steel blades, it is another showmanship next to pulled noodles. Compare with conventional noodle, it's thicker but the texture is very smooth. Topping of sliced noodle various, beef and lam are common choices.
